Discover More

At the helm of Shri Satyam Shivam Foundation stands Guruji Shri Amitji, a revered figure in the realm of holistic healing and spiritual guidance. With a profound expertise in Ashtanga Yoga and cosmic energy healing, Guruji Shri Amitji has dedicated over 18 years to the practice and mastery of Ashtanga Yoga, making him a seasoned practitioner in the field. His credentials include a remarkable journey of learning and enlightenment, with qualifications as a Master of Psychology, holding an M.D. in Alternative Therapies, and achieving the esteemed title of Reiki Grand Master and Marma Healing Master. His commitment to ancient healing arts extends to his proficiency as an expert Nadi Vaidya, specializing in the ancient art of pulse reading.

Under Guruji Shri Amitji's guidance, Shri Satyam Shivam Foundation is dedicated to spreading the timeless wisdom of Ayurveda and Marma Chikitsa, empowering individuals to embark on a transformative journey towards holistic wellness and spiritual awakening. With a profound understanding of the mind-body connection and the intricate balance of energy within, Guruji Shri Amitji brings forth a holistic approach to healing that encompasses physical, mental, and spiritual well-being. His teachings and therapies aim to alleviate suffering, restore harmony, and awaken the innate healing potential within each individual.
